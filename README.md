# Artifact-Aware Self-supervised Temporal Learning for Pediatric Lung Ultrasound Scoring
## Severity Assessment
•	Score 0—normal sliding, regular pleural line, and A-lines with fewer than 3 isolated B-lines;

•	Score 1— multiple well-defined B-lines or the range of coalescent B-lines <one intercostal with or without abnormal pleural line;

•	Score 2— the range of coalescent B-lines ≥one intercostal，or small subpleural consolidation (< 1 cm);

•	Score 3 — area of white lung or large consolidations (>1 cm)  without pleural effusion;
